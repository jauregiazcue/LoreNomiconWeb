LoreNomicon

First-person turn based adventure made in PS5

About

LoreNomicon was made by a group of 6 students in their third year at Sheffield Hallam University, each coming from either the games design, games art or computer science for games courses. It was made for their 3D games prototyping module and focuses on something called ‘Fakelore’, essentially, the group had the chance to make their game based off of their own unique folklore creatures, and explore what could be done with them. With the unique chance to work on the PS5 devkits, the group has spent the past few months working together to finish the project.
